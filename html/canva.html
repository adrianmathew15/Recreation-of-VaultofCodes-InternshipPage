  • Location: Virtual

About the Program:

Canva is a popular online graphic design tool that allows users to create a wide range of visual content with ease. It offers a user-friendly interface and a vast library of templates, images, icons, and fonts, making it accessible to both beginners and experienced designers. With Canva, individuals and businesses can design social media posts, presentations, posters, flyers, and more without the need for advanced design skills. The platform also supports collaboration, enabling teams to work together on projects in real-time. Whether for personal use or professional projects, Canva empowers users to unleash their creativity and produce stunning visuals effortlessly.

Key Highlights:

  1. Comprehensive Curriculum:

    Canva's curriculum is designed to help educators and students integrate graphic design skills into their learning experience. It offers a variety of educational resources, including lesson plans, activities, and tutorials, to teach design principles and creativity.

  2. Real-World Projects:

    Interns will have the opportunity to work on practical projects that mirror industry scenarios. This hands-on approach fosters creativity, problem-solving skills, and the ability to work in a team.

  3. Industry Insights:

    Engage with experienced mentors and professionals from the tech industry. Gain valuable insights into the latest trends, best practices, and career pathways in web development.

  4. Networking Opportunities:

    Connect with like-minded individuals, fellow interns, and professionals through regular meet-ups, virtual events, and collaborative projects. Build a network that could open doors to future opportunities.

  5. Feedback and Evaluation:

    Receive personalized feedback and evaluations from mentors, providing a roadmap for improvement and showcasing areas of strength.

Who Should Apply:

  • Enthusiastic individuals eager to jumpstart their career in canva designing.
  • Self-learners with a passion for designing.
  • Those looking to build a strong portfolio and gain practical experience.
